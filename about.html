Environmentalus: because our environment is ours, and protecting it is our responsibility

Welcome to my blog. I want to explore the interconnectedness of the economy and our environment. Some important questions I want to answer are:

  • How do our economic actions impact our surroundings?

  • Can it be easier to make environmentally-friendly and sustainable decisions?

  • Are today's developing countries doomed to grow in the same environmentally-damaging ways?
